Share your public key with your friends

Though the best possible way to share your public key with everyone would be to publish it on a keyserver, I suggest not doing this or doing this after you are a little used to the idea of using GPG keys. Instead I suggest you manually share your public key after exporting it.

To share your public key:

  1. Export it into a text file.

  2. Either email the contents of the file to your friends or send it as an attachment.

  3. Ask your friends to import the key into Mailvelope
