Import your keys into Mailvelope

To use Mailvelope with your keys, you must import your private key and the public keys of all the people you communicate with into Mailvelope. Your private key is imported and used to decrypt your messages and the public keys of all your friends are imported to encrypt messages to them. Both private and public keys are imported in the following manner.

To import your keys into Mailvelope:

  1. Select  ▸ Options.

  2. From the list of options on your left hand side, select Import Keys.

  3. Copy the contents of your key file into the textbox provided.

  4. Select Submit to finish. You can now select Display Keys from the list of options on your left side and check if your key has been imported.

To send encrypted email to your friends, import their public keys into Mailvelope. To decrypt received mails import your private key into Mailvelope.
