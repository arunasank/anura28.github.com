Secure your email communication

Once you have imported the public keys of your friends into Mailvelope, you can use it to encrypt email. To encrypt your mail:

  1. Sign into your Gmail account.

  2. Click the Compose button.

  3. Type in the message to be sent.

  4. Click the button. If you have not changed your Mailvelope preferences, a new Compose interface will open up.

  5. Select the text to be encrypted and click the button to select the key of your choice for encryption.

  6. A list of public and private keys will be available. Select the key of the email's recipient and click the Add button. This will add the key to the second window.

  7. Click the Ok button to encrypt your message with the selected key.

  8. Click the Transfer button to transfer the encrypted message to your Gmail Compose window.

  9. Finish by sending the message to your friend.
