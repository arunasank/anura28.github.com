Unlock your emails

Once you have imported your private keys into Mailvelope, you can read the email that your friends have encrypted with the corresponding public key.

To decrypt your messages

  1. Sign in to your Gmail account.

  2. Open the encrypted message.

  3. When you move your cursor over the encrypted message the message will be covered by a translucent screen with a lock. () When you move your cursor over the lock, a key will appear, as if to unlock the message. When the key appears, click.

  4. If the private key has been imported into Mailvelope, you will be prompted for the passphrase used to unlock the key. Type in the correct passphrase for the private key and click Ok.

  5. The encrypted message converted to plaintext will now be displayed.
